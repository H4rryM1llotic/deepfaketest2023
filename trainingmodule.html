What are Deepfakes?

Have you ever seen an image or a video that just isn't quite right? Perhaps a celebrity doing something out of character?

These are deepfakes.

Deepfakes are the next cyber security epidemic. The term comes from combining the words, deep learning and fake. They are made from an area of artifical intelligence called deep learning.
